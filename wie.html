Our Achievements

Panel Discussion on International Women's Day

Our WIE IEEE SIES GST Student Chapter celebrated Women's Day on 9th March 2020. On this occasion, a panel discussion on "Challenges faced by women in today's Social, Cultural and Work Setting" was arranged. Dr. Anu Binny (HR Technology Practitioner), Mrs. Neha Pasoria (Lawyer), Mrs. Rachna Sabu (Psychologist & Special Educator), Dr. Manisha S Abhyankar (HOD, Computer Science, KBP College) and Dr. Pooja Vyas Bansal (Obstetrics and Gynaecology) were the panelists from different fields.

STEM Activity

IEEE WIE conducted the STEM activity for the children of NMMC school no 15 for the 6th,7th and 8th grade.Advocating them about the field of science,technology and mathematics.

Benefits and Opportunities WIE affinity group

We organised a seminar conducted by WIE Bombay Section Chair,Prof. Seema Kawale on the benefits and opportunities WIE affinity group.

Got Any Idea is a national level online abstract submission and poster presentation competition in which the participants have to present the idea regarding a certain topic related to women empowerment, safety, development,etc.
